Renovate America Mobile App

The Problem

I joined the Benji team before the product was one year old. Benji operated as a lean startup within Renovate America and at the time only accepted credit applications over the phone. We needed to open a digital channel for accepting applications in order to scale the product while limiting increases to operational overhead.

The Process

I shadowed the customer service reps taking applications on the phone and did qualitative interviews with contractor sales reps to determine customer needs for a new application channel. The team decided to build a mobile app using Ionic framework so that the same codebase could be used to build both iOS and Android versions of the app (and ultimately be re-used for a web application).

I wireframed and prototyped a multi-step application and put it in front of some contractors to get quick feedback on what was working and what wasn't. After making a few content changes I worked closely with a visual designer to build out the final prototype.

The Outcome

We piloted the mobile app for about a month with several companies and sales reps and got very positive feedback before publicly launching the app on January 16, 2017. The web version of the application launched in March 2017 and by October 2017, web and mobile combined account for 60% of the incoming Benji applications and have resulted in over $40MM in funded loans.
